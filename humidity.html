You can see in the graph pictured that there is not a strong linear relationship between a city's latitude and its estimated humidity. There is a low to moderate positive linear relationship between the latitude of a city and its corresponding humidity. The relationship between these two is stronger in the northern hemisphere than in the southern hemisphere. In both, as latitude increases, humidity increases.
